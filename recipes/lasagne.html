Lasagne

Delicious minced meat with tomarotoes and honions

Hi people I'm back cooking again.. And today I'm going to be doing... Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 400gr minced meat
  • An onion
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Bits of salt
  • Pepper
  • A mixture of tomato sauce, tomato ketchup, tomato paste and every thing else
  • Tin of tomatoes

Steps

  1. Get a pan, pump some vegetable oil spray in there, give a couple pumps
  2. Put minced meat in the pan, take the paper off and break up the minced meat with an oofa doofa
  3. Cut up an onion
  4. Add onion to minced meat in pan
  5. Sprinkle some salt and pepper over it
  6. Add mixture og tomato sauce, tomato ketchup, tomato paste and everything else
  7. Give it a good mix
  8. Add a tin of tomatoes
  9. Put a lid on it and let it cook for a bit, for the honions to cook
  10. Get out the ovenproof dish and layer with minced meat, lasagna sheets and tomato sauce
  11. Put it in the oven for 10 minutes or so
  12. And that's Kay's Lasagne!
