Mexican Food

Delicious chicken in.. I don't know, some sauce, some spicy sauce

Hi people and I'm back cooking again.. And today I'm going to be doing... Mexican food in tacos

Ingredients

  • Some chicken
  • An onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Spicy sauce for the chicken
  • Soft taco shells
  • Taco sauce

Steps

  1. Cut up the onion
  2. Cut up the bell pepper
  3. Throw the onion and bell pepper in a frying pan
  4. Warm up the spicy sauce
  5. Throw the chicken in there and mix it up
  6. Ready up a soft taco shell
  7. Throw some chicken with spicy sauce in there
  8. Throw the cooked onion and bell pepper on top of the chicken
  9. Top it off with some taco sauce
  10. And that's mexican food in tacos!
