A different Spaghetti Bolognese

Minced meat on spaghetti sticks or whatever you wanna call 'em

Hi people and I'm back cooking..... Again. And today I'm going to be doin a different form of spaghetti bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400gr minced meat
  • 2 eggs
  • Spaghetti sticks
  • An onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Maybe we'll open a can of tomatoes

Steps

  1. First of all, we're gonna beat some eggs up
  2. And I got some spaghetti sticks here... Or whatever you wanna call em
  3. Right, I'm gonna put these egg in the.. Minced meat.. And I'm gonna mix the minced meat around
  4. And then what I'm going to do is.. Take me spaghetti sticks out.
  5. I don't even know how to do this.. I've seen it on video but I don't know how to do it..
  6. Grab the sticks.. Grab the soggy minced meat.. And put it around the sticks
  7. Then put it in the pan to go in the oven after putting soggy minced meat around 4 bundles of spaghetti sticks
  8. Pour some tomato sauce in there, as the guy in the video did it
  9. Remember to save some tomato sauce for after the first round of the oven
  10. Put them in the oven for about 20 minutes. Then we're gonna put the onion in
  11. Peel and cut an onion into pieces (this might make you cry)
  12. After the spaghetti sticks have been in the oven for 20 minutes, sprinkle the onion over
  13. Then top it with any spices of your choice
  14. Finish it off with the excess tomato sauce, and maybe open a tin of tomatoes
  15. I'm gonna open a tin of tomatoes as well, cuz it needs to be all covered
  16. Put it back in the oven for another half an hour or so, to cook the onions, then it should be OK.
  17. And that is a different Spag Bol!
